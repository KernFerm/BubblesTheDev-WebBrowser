Data Collection and Privacy Notice

BubblesTheDev is committed to protecting user privacy.

The BubblesTheDev Web Browser does **not** collect, transmit, sell, store, or share personal data, usage data, telemetry data, analytics data, browsing history, or device identifiers with BubblesTheDev or any third party.

Specifically:

* No telemetry services are embedded in the Software.
* No background analytics services are executed.
* No behavioral tracking mechanisms are included.
* No advertising identifiers are generated or transmitted.
* No cloud-based synchronization features are implemented.
* No artificial intelligence, machine learning, or remote inference systems are integrated into the Software.

All browsing activity remains local to the user’s device unless the user voluntarily interacts with external websites or third-party services through normal web browsing activity.

The Software does not establish outbound connections to BubblesTheDev-controlled servers for tracking or data collection purposes.

BubblesTheDev values user privacy and has intentionally designed this Software without built-in surveillance, telemetry, analytics, or AI-based monitoring components.

Any future feature that would materially change this privacy posture would be clearly disclosed in an updated license agreement and release documentation
